# Alpha ARC

# Sales Performance & Team Engagement Platform

# 🚀 Empower Sales Teams with Learning, Motivation & AI Insights

**An all-in-one platform for sales training, team collaboration, and performance tracking.**

Sales Performance & Team Engagement Platform is a cross-platform web and mobile solution with an integrated CRM, designed to help sales organizations improve communication, learning, motivation, and team performance. The platform combines educational content, daily engagement activities, AI-powered analytics, and gamification to create a productive and motivating environment for sales professionals.

## ✨ Features

- 👥 Team and user management
- 💬 CRM for sales team collaboration
- 🌟 Daily motivational quotes
- 🎥 Video uploads and sales training videos
- 📚 Weekly lessons and learning resources
- 🎧 Audio content for meditation, breathwork, and Daily Drops
- ✅ Daily Drop habit tracking
- 🔥 Streak and ARC score system
- 🌍 Time zone–based activity tracking
- 🤖 AI-powered engagement analytics
- 📊 Leaderboards and top performer rankings
- 🔔 Notifications and user engagement reminders
- 📱 Cross-platform web and mobile support
- 🎨 Clean, modern, and user-friendly interface

## 📱 About the Platform

The Sales Performance & Team Engagement Platform is built to support sales teams by combining training, motivation, and performance tracking into a single ecosystem. Managers can onboard team members, share learning materials, and monitor engagement, while users can build positive habits through Daily Drops, earn streaks, and improve their ARC scores.

Using AI-powered analytics, the platform tracks user activity, participation, and communication time to provide meaningful insights into team performance. Leaderboards recognize top performers, encouraging healthy competition and continuous personal growth.

The platform is ideal for:

- Sales organizations
- Team leaders and managers
- Business coaches
- Corporate training teams
- Sales professionals

## 🚀 How It Works

1. Create your account or join your organization.
2. Access daily quotes, training videos, and weekly lessons.
3. Complete Daily Drop habits based on your local time zone.
4. Listen to meditation, breathwork, and motivational audio sessions.
5. Earn streaks and increase your ARC score through consistent participation.
6. Track your performance and compare rankings on the leaderboard.
7. Managers monitor team engagement and growth through the CRM dashboard.

## 🛠️ CRM Features

The integrated CRM enables organizations to:

- 👥 Add and manage team members
- 📊 Monitor user engagement and activity
- 🎓 Share learning resources and training content
- 📈 Analyze team performance
- 🏆 Identify top-performing sales professionals

## 🎯 Why Use This Platform?

- Improve sales skills through continuous learning
- Keep teams motivated with daily content
- Build consistent habits with Daily Drops and streaks
- Gain AI-powered insights into user engagement
- Encourage healthy competition through leaderboards
- Manage teams efficiently from a centralized CRM
